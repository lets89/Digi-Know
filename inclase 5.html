Clase 5: Verbo “Have / Has” y posesivos

Objetivo

Expresar posesión usando el verbo have / has y los adjetivos posesivos.

Temas

Have / Has

  • I, you, we, they → have
  • He, she, it → has

Adjetivos posesivos

  • my – mi
  • your – tu
  • his – su (de él)
  • her – su (de ella)
  • its – su (de eso)
  • our – nuestro
  • their – su (de ellos)

Ejemplos

  • I have a cat.
  • She has a brother.
  • Their house is big.
  • We have a new teacher.
  • He has a red car.
